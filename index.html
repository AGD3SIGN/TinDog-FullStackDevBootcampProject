How It Works

1. Donate & Trade

Send us your pup’s gently used toys, leashes, or gear. Earn trade credits to use on refreshed goodies.

2. Refurbish & Refresh

3. Play It Forward

Pick new toys from our toy library, or get a surprise subscription box delivered monthly.
