How PupCycle Works

Our simple 4-step process transforms your dog's old toys into like-new treasures, keeping them out of landfills and your pup happy.

1

Send Your Toys

Pack up your dog's worn-out toys in our free shipping box and send them to our facility.

2

Clean & Refurbish

Our experts deep clean, sanitize, and repair each toy using eco-friendly processes.

3

Quality Check

Each toy passes our rigorous safety and quality inspection before approval.

4

Deliver Like New

Receive your refreshed toys or choose from our curated collection of recycled favorites.
